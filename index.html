Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions we get. If you have any other questions, please don't hesitate to contact us.

  • What is Bookmark?

    Bookmark is a simple and easy to use bookmarking service. Users can add, edit, delete and search for their favourite websites. Browse through your bookmarks and find the website you want to visit.

  • How can I request a new browser?

    We're working on adding more browsers to the list. If you're interested in adding it to your browser, please let us know. We're always happy to hear from you.

  • Is there a mobile app?

    Mobile apps are coming soon. We're working hard to make Bookmark available on all devices. Hopefully you'll be the first to know. Stay tuned.

  • What about other Chromium browsers?

    Chrome is the only browser we've tested. We're working hard to add more browsers to the list. If you're interested in adding it to your browser, please let us know. We're always happy to hear from you.
